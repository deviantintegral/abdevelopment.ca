Project Features: 
  • Quote database able to show random quotes on each page
  • Integrated photo management
  • Simple, clean, design
  • Contact form with anti-spam protection
  • XML Sitemap enabled for easy indexing by search engines
  • Easily extensible for future additions
Project Information
Project Date: 
January, 2008 - August, 2008
Project Website: 
healthyhumor.ca

"Healthy Humor" is the name of Dr. Ken Shonk's public speaking business. Establishing a simple website presence allows him to easily showcase his unique topics and extensive medical background. Healthy Humor is a portal allowing prospective clients the ability to find more about him and his services, as well as easily contact him for further information.
